Pet Putt

$date $time Rajin Shankar $location

Pet Putt is an adorable mini-golf game by GD@IU member Rajin Shankar featuring a wide cast of animal friends and hybrid puzzle-golf gameplay on spherical levels.

After just 3 months of development, Pet Putt has already seen positive reactions after a successful showcase at Indy PopCon 2019 where it was given the Best Aesthetic award by a panel of industry judges.

The game is currently in development (using the open-source Godot game engine), with a planned release during the first half of 2020. You can follow the game's development on Rajin's Twitter, and support it on Patreon! Patrons at all tiers also have access to all past, present, and future demo versions of the game.
