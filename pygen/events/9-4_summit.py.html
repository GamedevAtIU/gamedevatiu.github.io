IU Games Summit

9/4 6:00PM Franklin Hall Commons

Gamedev@IU is kicking off the Fall semester with the third annual IU Games Summit! You'll hear from us, as well as other game-related student organizations on the IU Bloomington Campus including Gaming@IU, Smash@IUB, Gamers' Guild, InGame, and the CEW&T Game Design Team.

Be sure to stop in and hear about our plans for the upcoming school year, talk to other game developers and players, and grab some free pizza.
